Frequently asked.
What will pricing look like after the founding member period?
Once founding member spots are filled, BakeFlo will move to a $14.99/month subscription — or $149/year (roughly two months free). Founding members pay nothing going forward. Ever.
What is the Founder's Deal?
You pay once — $97 — and get full access to BakeFlo forever. When we open to the public, it becomes a $14.99/month subscription. Founding members are locked in for life at no monthly cost.
Is this for hobby bakers or professional bakeries?
Both. BakeFlo works great for hobby bakers just starting to sell, and scales up to cottage bakeries and small custom shops managing multiple orders a week. If you're selling baked goods, you need to know your numbers.
Do I need to be tech-savvy?
Not at all. BakeFlo is designed to be simple and intuitive. If you can use a phone, you can use BakeFlo. Most bakers are up and running in under 10 minutes.
What devices does it work on?
BakeFlo works on any device — desktop, laptop, tablet, or phone. Just open your browser and go. No app store download required. On iPhone, tap Share → Add to Home Screen and BakeFlo opens like a native app — no download needed.
What happens after I purchase?
You'll get immediate access to your BakeFlo account at bakeflo.co. Your pantry comes pre-loaded with common baking ingredients so you can start pricing right away — and you can add any custom ingredients unique to your recipes.
Is there a guarantee?
Yes. You have 30 days from purchase to try BakeFlo risk-free. If it's not for you, just email us and we'll refund your full payment — no questions asked. We're confident that once you see your real costs and true margin, you won't want to go back.
If I cancel, what happens to my data?
Your recipes, pantry, and orders are always yours. You can export your data at any time. We never hold your information hostage — if you decide to leave, you leave with everything you built.
