# 🧁 Baker's Hub Pro — Deploy Guide

## How to get this live on the internet in ~20 minutes

---

### STEP 1 — Create a free GitHub account
1. Go to **github.com**
2. Click "Sign up" and create your account
3. Verify your email

---

### STEP 2 — Upload this project to GitHub
1. Once logged in, click the **+** button (top right) → "New repository"
2. Name it: `bakers-hub-pro`
3. Leave it set to **Public**
4. Click **"Create repository"**
5. On the next screen, click **"uploading an existing file"**
6. Drag the entire **bakers-hub** folder contents into the upload area
   - Make sure you upload ALL files including the `src/` and `public/` folders
7. Click **"Commit changes"**

---

### STEP 3 — Create a free Vercel account
1. Go to **vercel.com**
2. Click "Sign Up" → choose **"Continue with GitHub"**
3. Authorize Vercel to access your GitHub

---

### STEP 4 — Deploy!
1. In Vercel, click **"Add New Project"**
2. Find your `bakers-hub-pro` repository and click **"Import"**
3. Leave all settings as default
4. Click **"Deploy"**
5. Wait about 2 minutes ⏳
6. Vercel gives you a live URL like: `bakers-hub-pro.vercel.app` 🎉

---

### STEP 5 — Save to your iPad home screen
1. Open **Safari** on your iPad (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2. Go to your Vercel URL
3. Tap the **Share button** (box with arrow pointing up)
4. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Name it **Baker's Hub** and tap **Add**

It now lives on your home screen like a real app! Opens full screen, no browser bar. ✅

---

### Need a custom domain? (optional)
In Vercel → your project → Settings → Domains
You can add a custom domain like `bakershubpro.com` for ~$12/year from Namecheap or GoDaddy.
